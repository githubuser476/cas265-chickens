Terms

Cocks or roosters:
adult male chickens over the age of one year
Cockerels:
males less than a year old
Capons:
castrated roosters
Hens:
females over a year old, although in the egg-laying industry, a pullet becomes a hen when she begins to lay eggs at 16 to 20 weeks of age
Pullets:
younger females
Chook /ˈtʃʊk/:
all ages and both sexes
Chicks:
the young
